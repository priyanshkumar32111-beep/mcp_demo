# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project: Online Bookstore

### Overview
The Online Bookstore project aims to provide a platform for users to browse, search, and purchase books online.

### Business Goals
- Increase book sales by 30% in the next year.
- Provide a seamless online shopping experience.
- Enable users to review and rate books.

### Requirements

#### 1. User Registration & Login
- Users must be able to register with email and password.
- Users can log in and log out securely.

#### 2. Book Catalog
- Users can browse a catalog of books by category, author, and rating.
- Users can search for books by title or author.

#### 3. Shopping Cart & Checkout
- Users can add books to a shopping cart.
- Users can remove books from the cart.
- Users can complete a purchase using credit card or PayPal.

#### 4. Order History
- Users can view their past orders.

#### 5. Reviews & Ratings
- Users can leave reviews and ratings for books they have purchased.

### Acceptance Criteria
- All user data is stored securely.
- The system is available 99.9% of the time.
- Payment processing is PCI compliant.
